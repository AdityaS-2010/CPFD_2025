Welcome to the High School Course Planner

This website helps you create a personalized 4-year high school course plan that meets graduation requirements and prepares you for college admissions (including UC/CSU eligibility).

What can you do here?

  • Plan your courses: Select your CTE pathway, math, science, English, social science, fine art, and language preferences.
  • Auto-generate a 4-year plan: The planner will automatically fill in your schedule based on your choices and graduation/UC/CSU requirements.
  • Download your plan: Save your personalized course plan as a PDF to share with counselors or parents.
  • Chat with our AI: Get instant answers to your questions and personalized advice on course selection.
