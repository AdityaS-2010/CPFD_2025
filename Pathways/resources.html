How to Use the Course Planner Website

Welcome! This page will guide you through the main features of the course planner and how to get the most out of it.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Select Your Pathway:
    Choose your CTE pathway (e.g., Business, Engineering, Biomed, ICT, Education) from the dropdown menu at the top of the planner page.
  2. Customize Your Preferences:
    Use the dropdowns to select your math, English, science, social science, fine art, and language tracks. If you play a sport or take a language outside of school, be sure to indicate that for a more accurate plan.
  3. Generate Your 4-Year Planner:
    Click the Generate Planner button. The website will automatically fill in your schedule, ensuring you meet graduation and college eligibility requirements.
  4. Review & Edit:
    You can manually edit any course in the planner. The system will highlight any unrecognized courses in red and suggest corrections.
  5. Validate Your Plan:
    The planner will show if you are missing any requirements for graduation or UC/CSU eligibility. Adjust your plan as needed.
  6. Download as PDF:
    Once satisfied, click Download Planner as PDF to save or print your plan.
  7. Ask the Chatbot:
    Use the Open Chat button to ask questions about course planning. The chatbot can help with requirements, course options, and more.
  8. Reset Planner:
    Click Reset Planner to clear all selections and start over.

Tips & Features

  • All course names are auto-corrected as you type. If a course is not recognized, it will be highlighted.
  • Conditional questions (like ENS4 or language) will appear only if relevant to your selections.
  • All your choices are private and not saved on the server.
  • Use the Back to Home button to return to the main page at any time.
